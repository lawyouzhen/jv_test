Financial Planning

We help you set goals, manage investments, and grow your wealth.

Financial planning involves managing income, expenses, savings, investments, and insurance to help individuals or families achieve their financial goals and maintain financial health.
Planning helps allocate resources wisely, ensures future financial stability and security, prepares for potential economic risks, and supports long-term goals such as retirement, education, and home ownership.

Financial planning include ：

  • Family Trust
  • Business Trust
  • Living Trust

Healthcare Planning

We help you plan for immediate and long-term health needs.

Healthcare planning involves organizing and managing personal or family healthcare needs, including selecting appropriate insurance, setting up emergency medical funds, and planning for long-term care.
Healthcare planning ensures access to necessary medical services in case of health issues, reduces the financial impact of medical expenses, and supports long-term health and well-being.

Healthcare planning include :

  • Life Insurance (Term, Whole Life, Investment-linked)
  • Medical Insurance (Individual / Group Medical Insurance, Critical Illness Insurance)
  • Accident Insurance (Individual / Travel / Family Accident Insurance)

Legacy Planning

We assist with wills, trusts, and charitable giving to preserve your assets.

Legacy planning is the process of making arrangements during one’s lifetime to ensure that assets are passed on according to personal wishes after death, while minimizing tax burdens and legal disputes.
Legacy planning helps preserve family wealth, ensures fair distribution of assets, prevents family disputes over inheritance, and effectively reduces estate taxes.

Legacy planning include :

  • Will

Claim Support

We guide you through the process to ensure you receive your entitled benefits.

Claim support involves assisting clients through the insurance claim process, including preparing claim documents, conducting claim analysis, supporting negotiations, and providing legal and financial advice.
Claim support increases the chances of successful claims, ensures clients receive the compensation they deserve, reduces complexity and stress in the claims process, and protects clients' legal rights.

Claim Support include :

  • Life Insurance (Term, Whole Life, Investment-linked)
  • Medical Insurance (Individual / Group Medical Insurance, Critical Illness Insurance)
  • Accident Insurance (Individual / Travel / Family Accident Insurance)
